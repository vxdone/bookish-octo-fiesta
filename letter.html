dear olivia,

it's been quite awhile since we last talked, so let's just have a chat.

i hope you're faring well in... michigan? california? i honestly don't know where you are at the moment lol. hopefully it isn't somewhere too banal for your liking.

i haven't been the most active onine (which you probably have noticed) lately. i've been doing some reflecting and musing over my life choices this summer, so that's why i've been i/a.

i've come to realize that you're one of the few who truly care about me. you've been there for me during times of dispair, and i'm so grateful for that. in fact, you may have been the only one who i relied on.

so, thank you. truly. i miss you terribly over here. it doesn't really feel the same anymore. i guess i got too used to your comforting presence.

at the time you're reading this, i'm guessing you haven't started school yet. because, let's be honest, who starts school in early august? (ok maybe boarding schools, but i don't think you're attending one... right???). hope the school environment isn't too stressful and discomforting. i know new schools can be a bit daunting at times.

i'm a bit scared to start my school year, but i know i have great friends like you that i can rely on. i'll try my best to be more active online, but life may get in the way lol.

anyways! enough about me. how are you actually doing? how's the weather? is it snowing right now (i never actually experienced snow, so i consider you a lucky person)?

wait - how's your birthday going? i am just now realizing i have rambled on for about 2/3 of this letter and haven't really mentioned your birthday at all (oopsies). forgive me. have you eaten cake? gotten together with family? recieved any cool presents?

maybe one day we'll meet up again and celebrate your birthday in person. i look forward to that day. it was fun hanging out with you that one rainy day during 8th grade. (remember when my mom said something along the lines of kidnapping u when she first saw you? LOL)

ok ok, i'll stop rambling now. go ahead and enjoy your birthday. you deserve it. if you need anything, shoot me a message. i'll try my best to respond.

cheers to another year of this friendship,

laura :]
